Google LLC is an American multinational corporation and technology company focusing on online advertising, search engine technology, cloud computing, computer software, quantum computing, e-commerce, consumer electronics, and artificial intelligence (AI).

Google was founded on September 4, 1998, by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were PhD students at Stanford University. Together they own about 14% of its publicly-listed shares and control 56% of the stockholder voting power through super-voting stock.

Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. The company's flagship product, the Google Search engine, handles billions of search queries daily.
